Campus Life

The schoolday starts at 8:25. There are 8 periods, a lunch, and an extra period. Each period is 45 minutes long. ILA is 2 periods. There is an A lunch for 6th graders, a B lunch for 7th graders, and a C lunch for 8th graders.
